The Ngaji Yok application ("the Application") is provided by Usman ("we," "us," or "the Service Provider") as an Ad-Supported Free service. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information, with a strict commitment to transparency and user privacy.

1. Information Collection and Use

While the Application is built with a "Privacy-First" architecture, we use specific third-party services to provide core functionalities and maintain the service.

A. Precise Location Data

  • Purpose: To calculate accurate Islamic prayer times and determine the Qibla direction based on your exact position.
  • Handling: Coordinates are processed locally on your device. While stored in the device's DataStore for offline use, this data is never uploaded to our personal servers.
  • Third-Party: Shared with Google Play Services only for geocoding purposes (identifying city names for display).

B. Device Identifiers & Analytics

To improve user experience and app stability, we use Firebase (Google Inc.):

  • Firebase Analytics: Tracks anonymous usage patterns (e.g., how often the Quran is read).
  • Firebase Remote Config: Manages app versions and mandatory updates.
  • Firebase In-App Messaging: Displays important religious announcements or quotes.
  • Data Collected: Device IDs, app version, and basic interaction logs.

C. Advertising & Consent Management (AdMob & UMP)

The Application displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob.

  • Consent Management (GDPR Compliance): In accordance with Google's latest requirements, we have implemented the User Messaging Platform (UMP) SDK. For users in the European Economic Area (EEA) and the UK, this system manages your preferences for data collection and personalized advertising.
  • Data Collected: AdMob may collect advertising IDs (e.g., Android Advertising ID), IP addresses, and device information to serve ads, prevent fraud, and limit ad frequency.
  • User Choice: You can manage or revoke your consent at any time through the "Consent" dialog in the app settings or via your Android device settings (Google > Ads).

D. Sensor Data

  • Magnetometer & Accelerometer: Used exclusively for the Qibla Finder feature. This data is processed in real-time and is never recorded, stored, or transmitted.

2. Device Permissions (Android 14 & 15 Compliance)

The Application requires the following permissions to function reliably on modern Android versions:

SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM To trigger Adzan alerts exactly at prayer times.
FOREGROUND_SERVICE (Media Playback) To ensure uninterrupted Murottal (Quran audio) and Adzan playback.
POST_NOTIFICATIONS To deliver prayer reminders and "Mutiara Hikmah" quotes.
ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION To calculate prayer times based on your current location.
RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED To restore prayer alarms automatically after a device restart.

3. Third-Party Services

We integrate with the following trusted providers:

  • Google AdMob & UMP SDK Privacy Policy
  • Google Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Google Play Services Privacy Policy
  • EveryAyah.com Public repository used for streaming Quran audio. No personal data is shared with this provider.

4. Data Security & Storage

  • App Sandbox: Your worship progress (Bookmarks, Amaliah records) is stored within the Android App Sandbox, making it inaccessible to other applications.
  • No Personal Info: We do not collect names, phone numbers, email addresses (unless you contact us), or financial information.

5. External Links

The Application may contain links to external sites (e.g., religious references). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external websites.

6. Children's Privacy

Ngaji Yok is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal identifiable information from children under 13. AdMob is configured to comply with family-friendly ad standards and COPPA.

7. User Control

  • Revoking Access: You can disable location or notification access at any time in Android Settings.
  • Data Deletion: Uninstalling the Application or using "Clear Data" in Android Settings will permanently erase all local worship records and settings.
